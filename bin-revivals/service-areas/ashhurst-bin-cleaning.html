The Reality

Nobody Wants to Clean Their Own Bins

And honestly — why would you? Here's why most households leave it to us.

It's Unpleasant

The grime, the smell, the bacterial residue — cleaning a badly neglected bin is genuinely unpleasant work. It's a job that requires gloves, old clothes and a strong stomach. Most people avoid it for exactly this reason.

It Takes Real Time

Doing it properly — not just a quick rinse — means setting aside 30–45 minutes with the right cleaning gear. That's time most Ashhurst households simply don't want to spend on bin cleaning.

Needs the Right Equipment

A garden hose and some dish soap doesn't cut it for a bin that's been accumulating bacteria for months. Professional results require professional equipment — high-pressure washing, proper sanitisation solutions and deodorising treatments.
